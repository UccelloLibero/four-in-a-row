Welcome to Connect 4!

Drop your tokens, connect four, and outsmart your opponent!

How to play:

  • Player 1 uses red tokens.
  • Player 2 uses yellow tokens.
  • Drop your tokens using **left** and **right** arrow keys, then press **down** to place it.
  • The first to connect **four in a row** (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins!
  • Play as many times as you want. Just hit "Play Again" when the game ends!
